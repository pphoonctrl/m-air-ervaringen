Why this website?

This website was created after I spent months trying to get the Smart M-Air app from Mitsubishi Heavy Industries to function reliably, without success.

Over the past few months, I have dedicated more than 100 hours to researching, testing, configuring, and even completely replacing my home network. Despite all these efforts, the exact same issues kept returning.


During my search, I discovered that I am definitely not alone. Users worldwide have been reporting similar problems for years, ranging from slow response times and connection failures to login issues and disconnecting devices.


With this website, I want to combine user experiences from the Netherlands and abroad, so consumers are better informed and suppliers/manufacturers are encouraged to implement structural improvements.

Common Issues

Analysis of Smart M-Air Experiences

This page bundles public user experiences, forum discussions, and app store reviews regarding the Smart M-Air app. The goal is to provide a structured overview of recurring patterns.


Frequently Mentioned Issues

Connection Dropping

Many users report that the AC regularly loses connection or shows "no communication".

Login Errors

A recurring issue is being logged out unexpectedly or losing paired devices.

Slow Cloud Response

Commands via the app are often executed with a delay or fail completely.

Multi-user Conflicts

When multiple family members use the same account, synchronization errors occur.

Evolution of Complaints Over the Years

Based on public reviews, a striking pattern emerges: the nature of the complaints has barely changed over the years.

2019 – First Large-Scale Complaints
  • Wi-Fi registration issues with the WF-RAC module
  • Unstable connection with routers
2020 - 2021 – Cloud Issues Become Prominent
  • Delays in remote operation
  • Time-outs when executing commands
2022 - 2023 – Multi-user & Account Problems
  • Users unexpectedly logged out
  • Family members disrupting each other's sessions
2024 - 2025 – Modern Network Compatibility
  • Difficulties with mesh networks and modern routers
  • Errors after official firmware updates
2026 – Issues Remain Fully Active
  • Long loading times, units disappearing from the app, and connection timeouts.

Problems with Smart M-Air? Recommended Steps

If you are experiencing persistent issues, follow these guidelines to build a case with your supplier:

Step 1 – Report the issue in writing

Notify your installer or dealer via email and request a fix. Keep records of all communications.

Step 2 – Allow time for repair

Give the supplier a reasonable timeframe to investigate and resolve the connection issues.

Step 3 – Document everything

Take videos of the app failing, note dates, and take screenshots of error codes.
